Corporate culture

We build for practical efficiency, emotional comfort, and durable trust.

Reliable Helper Gia My is a team that treats utility apps as part of everyday life. We do not chase feature volume for its own sake. We focus on what users truly need, then shape the experience with thoughtful design, disciplined engineering, and a human tone.

Guiding belief

Make everyday life more efficient and more warm.

That belief shapes both our internal culture and our products. We care about smoother routines, lighter decision load, clearer interfaces, and software that feels considerate instead of overwhelming.

User Needs at the Center

We start by identifying real-world scenarios and unmet needs, then align product decisions around that truth.

Careful Craft

Every utility app is polished through iteration so the final experience feels clear, stable, and intuitive.

Useful Delight

We pair functional productivity gains with moments of warmth, calm, and subtle enjoyment that make tools feel alive.

Work style

One product mindset across research, design, code, release, and support.

Our culture rejects handoff fragmentation. Designers think about launch conditions. Engineers consider long-term usability. Product thinking remains present from concept mapping to compliance maintenance. That continuity helps us move faster with fewer blind spots.

We balance two distinct product visions: Prism Life, which helps users boost productivity and achieve self-growth, and PopTooly, which adds joy and convenience to everyday life. Both are driven by the same philosophy 鈥?genuine utility with genuine warmth.

What we encourage inside the team

Clarity, accountability, empathy, and global awareness.

Because our products serve users across Europe and North America, our internal standards account for international expectations around usability, privacy, accessibility, and respectful monetization.

Clear Thinking

We reduce noise, define the problem precisely, and prefer decisions that can be defended by user value.

Honest Collaboration

We challenge assumptions directly and improve ideas through rigorous discussion instead of vague agreement.

Responsible Delivery

Compliance, privacy, age-appropriate design, and marketplace standards are treated as product quality issues.

Long-Term Product Care

We build products that can mature over time rather than chasing short-lived attention spikes.
